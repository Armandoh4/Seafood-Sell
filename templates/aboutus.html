About Us

At Seafood Sell, we are passionate about bringing the freshest and finest seafood from the ocean to your table. Based in the heart of London, our mission is to provide our customers with high-quality, sustainably sourced seafood that delights the palate and supports a healthy lifestyle.

Our Story
Founded in 2023, Seafood Sell has quickly become a trusted name in the seafood industry. Our journey began with a simple idea: to offer Londoners access to the best seafood available, sourced responsibly and delivered with exceptional service. Over the years, we have built strong relationships with local fishermen and international suppliers, ensuring that our seafood is always fresh, flavorful, and ethically sourced.
